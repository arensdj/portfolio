About Me

The very first time I wrote code was in high school where I coded a simple program in Basic. This fascinated me. My curiosity led me to study Computer Science. I worked in the tech industry for seven years as a system administrator and software developer. The project I supported produced training and maintenance manuals used by airline pilots, customers and mechanics. I interfaced extensively with my customers to ensure that I working towards accomplishing our company mission.

When I'm not coding, I take great pleasure working in my garden planting various trees, shrubs and flowers. My garden is my santuary when I need a place to relax. I also enjoy hiking, camping and cycling with my family. Many years ago I had the opportunity to hike the Grand Canyon. This was such an awesome experience that I will never forget.
As a life long learner, I have recently gained experience in Java and JavaScript programming. Soon I plan to learn to code in Python as I continue to build my knowledge and skillset in web development.
